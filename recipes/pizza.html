Who doesn't love pizza? If you are still here thinking you are going to just order a pizza and sit on your lazy ass and wait for it to fall on your face to eat you are wrong. Get your ass up and start making this delicious pizza before you die from a heart attack. This pizza is to die for. Literally.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Knead the dough and shape into circle
  2. Distibute the pizza sauce evenly throughout the dough
  3. Pour cheese over pizza sauce
  4. Add Pepperoni
  5. Bake at 800 degrees
  6. Eat
