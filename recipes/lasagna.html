Anyway, just make it already

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a baking dish
  2. Combine the meat, tomato sauce and put it on top of the pasta
  3. Pour cheese over meat and tomato sauce
  4. Repeat until all layers are done
  5. Bake at 400 degrees
  6. Eat
