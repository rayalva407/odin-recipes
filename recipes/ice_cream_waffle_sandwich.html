Eggo Waffle Ice Cream Sandwich

This is the future of dessert

The name really should say it all. Who came up with this idea? Nobody knows but we have to thank that amazing person for doing such a service to humanity. Let's worship the eggo waffle ice cream sandwich and abide by its rules. The sandwich has spoken. It's time to eat.

Ingredients

  • Eggo Waffles
  • Ice Cream

Steps

  1. Get the eggo waffles and prepare them in the toaster
  2. After the waffles are cooked let rest until cool
  3. Once waffles are cooled smear a liberal amount of ice cream on them
  4. Assemble the sandwich with second waffle
  5. Praise the holy waffle
  6. Munch
